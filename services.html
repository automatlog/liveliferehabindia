What types of addiction do you treat?

We provide treatment for alcohol, drugs, nicotine, and other substance addictions with tailored rehabilitation programs.

How long is the rehabilitation program?

The duration depends on the severity of addiction; typically, programs range from 21 days to 90 days or more with personalized care plans.

Do you provide medical supervision?

Yes, our experienced medical team supervises all patients throughout the detoxification and recovery process to ensure safety and well-being.

Can families be involved in therapy?

Absolutely, family counseling and therapy sessions are an integral part of our rehabilitation programs to support recovery and communication.

Do you offer outpatient programs?

Yes, we have outpatient programs and follow-up sessions to ensure continuous support after inpatient treatment.
