Mobile De-Addiction Treatment & Digital Detox Program

Program Type:
Mobile & Digital De-Addiction Program
Program Duration:
21 to 60 Days (Based on Assessment)
Focus Area:
Digital Dependency, Screen Time Management & Mental Wellness
Support Offered:
Behavioral Therapy, Counseling, Lifestyle Coaching & Family Guidance

Mobile De-Addiction Program Overview:

Our Mobile De-Addiction Program helps individuals regain control over their digital habits and reduce excessive dependence on mobile phones, social media, and gaming. We focus on creating a healthier relationship with technology while promoting productivity and emotional balance.

At Nasha Mukti Kendra Vadodara, our team combines behavioral therapy, counseling, and digital detox methods to help participants break the cycle of compulsive mobile use. The goal is not only to limit screen time but also to restore focus, sleep quality, and real-world connections.

Our Approach:

Assessment & Awareness:
  • We start with an in-depth assessment to evaluate the extent of mobile or internet dependency.
  • Clients gain awareness of how digital habits affect mental health, productivity, and relationships.
Digital Detox & Counseling:
  • We provide structured digital detox sessions supported by counseling and lifestyle modification plans.
  • Therapists help participants manage withdrawal symptoms, anxiety, and boredom associated with reduced phone usage.
Behavioral & Lifestyle Therapy:
  • We introduce productive routines, physical activities, and mindfulness techniques to replace screen habits.
  • Behavioral therapy helps modify unhealthy thought patterns and builds self-discipline.
Family & Aftercare Support:
  • We involve families to create a supportive environment and set healthy digital boundaries at home.
  • Aftercare includes regular follow-ups, relapse prevention counseling, and digital wellness workshops.

Results

  • Reduced Screen Time: Participants reported a 60–80% reduction in unnecessary mobile use within weeks.
  • Improved Focus: Clients experienced better concentration, productivity, and sleep quality.
  • Enhanced Relationships: Families noticed stronger real-world connections and reduced digital conflicts.

Conclusion

Our Mobile De-Addiction Program helps individuals regain control over technology and restore balance in their lives. At Nasha Mukti Kendra Vadodara, we believe that digital wellness is essential for mental, emotional, and social health — and our expert team is dedicated to guiding every participant toward mindful, healthy technology use.
