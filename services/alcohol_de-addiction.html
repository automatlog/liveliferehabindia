Alcohol De-Addiction Treatment & Recovery Program

Program Type:
Alcohol De-Addiction Treatment
Program Duration:
30 to 90 Days (Based on Assessment)
Treatment Focus:
Alcohol De-Addiction & Rehabilitation
Support Offered:
Counseling, Detox, Therapy & Aftercare

Alcohol De-Addiction Treatment Overview:

Our Alcohol De-Addiction Program is designed to help individuals break free from alcohol dependency and regain control of their lives. We provide a structured, compassionate environment that promotes healing and long-term recovery.

At Nasha Mukti Kendra Vadodara, our expert team uses a blend of medical supervision, counseling, therapy, and support systems to ensure a holistic approach to recovery. Each patient’s treatment plan is tailored to their individual needs and challenges.

Our Approach:

Personalized Assessment:
  • We begin with a detailed assessment of the individual’s physical, mental, and emotional health.
  • This helps us create a customized recovery plan that ensures the best outcomes.
Detoxification & Medical Care:
  • Our medically supervised detox process helps remove alcohol toxins from the body safely.
  • We prioritize comfort, safety, and 24/7 medical monitoring during this critical stage.
Therapy & Counseling:
  • We provide individual and group counseling sessions to address the emotional and psychological aspects of addiction.
  • Therapies include cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), motivational sessions, and mindfulness-based support.
Aftercare & Support:
  • After successful completion of the treatment, we provide ongoing aftercare programs.
  • This includes regular counseling, relapse prevention, and family support sessions to ensure lasting recovery.
